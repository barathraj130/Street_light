System Overview

Intelligent Infrastructure

Developed and tested by Fluxora Technology in Tiruppur, Tamil Nadu. Our system leverages STM32-based intelligence and long-range connectivity to create a responsive, energy-saving lighting network.

Motion Intelligence

Ultrasonic sensors detect movement, automatically dimming or brightening lights based on real-time traffic.

LoRa Connectivity

Long-range, low-power communication ensures seamless data transfer across entire city districts.

BH1750 Lux Sensor

Precise light intensity detection ensures lights only activate when natural light falls below required levels.
